Who We Are - CCF Sandoval

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples and planting churches to reach families, communities, and nations for Christ.

We are committed to creating a welcoming environment where every person feels valued and inspired to grow in their faith journey.

Our Vision

A movement of Spirit-filled believers transforming lives and communities through the gospel of Jesus Christ.

We believe in serving our communities with excellence and love, extending God's grace to all people.

Serving the Sandoval Community

Located in Sandoval, Pasig, we are part of the larger Christ's Commission Fellowship movement, dedicated to transforming lives and impacting our community for Christ's glory.
