CCF Sandoval Ministries

NxtGen Ministry

Building Strong Foundations in Christ

NxtGen Ministry partners with parents to disciple children and youth by teaching them to know Christ, love God’s Word, and live out biblical values at an early age.

Elevate Ministry

Helping Students Follow Christ Faithfully

Elevate Ministry is a Christ-centered community that equips high school and college students to grow in their faith, develop godly character, and influence others for Christ.

Big Ministry

Strengthening Marriages and Families for God’s Glory

Big Ministry disciples couples and families to build Christ-centered homes by applying biblical principles in marriage, parenting, and everyday family life.

Across Ministry

Making Christ Known Across Cultures and Nations

Across Ministry fulfills the Great Commission by sending, supporting, and equipping disciples to reach communities, cultures, and nations with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Shepherd Staff Ministry

Caring for God’s People with Biblical Counsel

Shepherd Staff Ministry provides pastoral care, counseling, and spiritual guidance to help individuals and families grow in Christ and navigate life’s challenges biblically.
